My Focus

I am a physiotherapist who has dedicated my working career to the treatment of babies and children with difficulties in posture, movement, and development. I feel great responsibility in helping young children in their motoric development as this is a very crucial period in their lives. Physical abilities acquired at young age are essential for an independent life in adulthood.

About me

My name is Anette Jonischkeit. I am a physiotherapist with more than 30 years experience in the treatment of babies and children. I am passionate about and dedicated to helping children reach their full potential.

My Approach

Together with parents and carers, we work on maximising the personal abilities of every child to give them all the chances they deserve.

Read More

I provide services for children with:

  • Developmental delay (e.g. floppy infant syndrome, hypermobility, low muscle tone)
  • Neurological motor disturbances (e.g. cerebral palsy, syndromes, brain injury)
  • Orthopaedic problems (e.g. torticollis, scoliosis, hip dysplasia, talipes, flatfoot)
  • Peripheral lesions (e.g. Erb's palsy, spina bifida, spinal cord injury)
  • Respiratory problems (e.g. cystic fibrosis, asthma, chronic lung disease)
