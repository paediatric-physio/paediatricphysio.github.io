Treatment times and Fees:

Duration of a treatment session

Usually a treatment session for children with a complex, chronic or neurological condition is one hour. This allows enough time for the child to settle in and for me to assess the child, discuss goals and relevant treatment matters and perform and the treatment. The first session includes a comprehensive assessment, goal setting and the start of the treatment. This might take up to 1.5 hours. Sessions for orthopaedic or less complex conditions usually take 30-45 minutes.

    Fees:

  • 30 min session: $95
  • 45 min session: $142.50
  • 60 min session: $190
  • Comprehensive written report $190-$475

    Cancellation policy:

  • If you are unable to attend a session, please cancel your appointment as early as possible at least 24 hours before your appointment. A $50 cancellation fee may apply if the appointment is not attended and not cancelled on time.
  • For weeklong treatments for interstate clients please cancel your appointments as early as possible; at least one week in advance. A $150 cancellation fee may be charged if these multiple appointments are not attended and not cancelled on time.

    • Please note that due to their funding guidelines a cancellation fee can’t be billed through Better Start.
